Scenario: The Call

Your phone rings. A calm voice says:
"Hi, this is Alex from IT. There's malware on your machine. I need access to fix it remotely."

Alex responds:
"Sure, totally understand. You can call me back at extension 314. I’m working with Susan in HR— she asked me to fix this ASAP. The malware could spread if we don’t act fast."

“Alex” calls again:
“I never got a call back. This could lock your whole department out. Just approve the request in your inbox and I’ll take care of it.”

You receive an email from your manager:
“I’m tied up in meetings. Please respond to the IT request from earlier and give them what they need. –Sarah”
